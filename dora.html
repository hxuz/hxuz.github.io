Project context

The Products surface serves as the pricing page for logged-in users, with over 90% of visitors being existing paid users.

I redesigned this surface by leveraging users' plan and usage data to create a more personalized product exploration experience. This targeted approach better addresses specific user needs, resulting in improved conversion.

I also developed new growth design components that can be reused in future experiments and designs. This not only expedites engineering timelines but also reduces costs for future initiatives.
